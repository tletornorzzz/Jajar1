During the camp, I learned about cosmetic formulation, laboratory practices, and the scientific principles behind beauty products. I believe that my passion, determination, and willingness to learn will allow me to make the most of the opportunities at King Mongkut's University of Technology North Bangkok. Thank you for considering my application. I sincerely hope to become a part of your university community and grow into a knowledgeable and responsible professional in the field of Health and Beauty Science.
